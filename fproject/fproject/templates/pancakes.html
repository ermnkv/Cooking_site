Pancakes

Tasty and fast.

RECIPE INGREDIENTS:

1. 1/3 cups (189g) all-purpose flour (preferably unbleached), scoop and level to measure
2. Tbsp (25g) granulated sugar
3. tsp baking powder
4. 1/4 tsp baking soda
5. 1/2 tsp salt
6. 1/3 cups whole milk
7. 3 Tbsp butter, melted (unsalted or salted is fine)
8. 1 large egg
9. 2 tsp plain vinegar or lemon juice
10. 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

STEP 1

In a large mixing bowl whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt for 20 seconds. Make a well in center of flour mixture, set aside. In a separate medium mixing bowl or large liquid measuring cup whisk together milk, egg, butter, vinegar or lemon juice. Pour milk mixture into well in flour mixture. Whisk just to blend, batter should be slightly lumpy. Let batter rest a few minutes to fluff up.

STEP 2

Preheat an electric griddle to 350 – 375 degrees depending on how hot your griddle gets. Grease griddle (with non-stick cooking spray or a little butter – but be quick if using butter). Pour about 1,3 cup batter at a time onto griddle. Spread batter out slightly if thick (rounds should be about 4 1,3-inches). Let cook until golden brown on bottom and bubbles appear on top surface, about 1 minute. Flip and cook opposite side until browned on bottom.

STEP 3

Serve warm with topping of choice.
